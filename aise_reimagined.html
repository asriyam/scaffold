Assumptions & sizing math

Open to scrutiny. Change any of these and the BOM ripples.
We assumed 70% peak GPU utilization, fp8 quantization on the 70B model, an 8K context window, RAG retrieval over a 240 TB indexed corpus, 30-day audio retention for ambient documentation, and 10:1 average compression ratio on cold-tier storage. Power assumes typical PUE of 1.4 in your existing data centers. Staffing model assumes 1 AI platform lead and 2 MLOps engineers shared across all three workloads. Public-cloud comparison assumes equivalent H100 instance hours at published list prices, plus egress and PHI-residency-compatible dedicated tenancy.
